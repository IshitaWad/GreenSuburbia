Let us introduce ourselves

Welcome to Green Suburbia, a free online carbon footprint calculator.

Green Suburbia was created by Anika Peer and Ishita Wadhwa to provide a holistic calculation of a family's carbon emissions in one calendar year.

The unique purpose of this calculator is to encourage users to reduce their carbon emissions by giving them personalized feedback and tips on their consumption of water, gas, electricity and fuel.
